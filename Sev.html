colosseum

The Colosseum, built in 80 CE, was a central venue for public entertainments like gladiatorial contests and animal hunts in ancient Rome.

pantheon

Built by Agrippa between 25 and 27 BC the Pantheon was a temple dedicated to the twelve Gods and to the living Sovran.

palatine hill

Believed to have been inhabited since 1000 BC, Palatine Hill is considered the most important and famous of the seven hills of Rome.
